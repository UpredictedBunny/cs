💡 Weight Gain & Study Tips

🥛 Never skip mealsEven if you're not hungry, eat. Set phone alarms for every meal. Consistent caloric surplus is the only way to gain weight.
🍌 Calorie-dense foodsFocus on: peanut butter, full-fat dairy, eggs, rice, ghee, nuts, dates, avocado, bananas. These pack maximum calories.
💪 Light exercise20-min walks stimulate appetite. Push-ups/bodyweight exercises 3x/week build muscle (muscle weighs more than fat).
😴 Sleep is medicineGrowth hormone releases during deep sleep. 6.5–7 hrs minimum every night. Sleep is when you actually gain weight.
📖 Study in 90-min blocksPomodoro technique: 45 min study → 10 min break → repeat. Two cycles = 90 min session. Most efficient method.
🕌 Prayer = mental resetEach Namaz is a built-in 5-min break. Use it to fully disconnect from study. You'll return more focused.
💧 Drink after mealsDon't drink water right before eating — it fills your stomach. Drink 20–30 min after each meal.
📋 Weekly trackingWeigh yourself every Sunday morning. If no gain after 2 weeks, increase meal portions. Slow progress is still progress.
